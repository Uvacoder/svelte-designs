About

What is this? Well, me and my friend are doing this challenge where we try to implement 3 designs each week into code. Feel free to submit your design to us btw.
Why is it called "Three of {Some,Every}thing"? It came up by accident actually. The first time I make the repo for this challenge on github, I don't know what to name it. So I was thinking, because we implement 3 designs each week, I want the word "three" for its name. Being me who isn't really good at naming stuff so I just went with Three of Something. Then a few moments later, my friend made his repo called Three of Everything. If we combine those, we'll get Three of {Some,Every}thing. And yes, that's BASH expansion braces.
